About me

Hello world! I am Budi. I graduated from Universitas Gadjah Mada with bachelor of network engineering in 2019, right before COVID-19 strikes. During pandemic, I've nothing much to do so I decided to learn web development from the Internet and join Fullstack JavaScript Bootcamp at Hacktiv8.

I really enjoy learning about making websites, especially the frontend part. It's like being the architect and artist of the online world, ensuring that websites not only function well but also leave a lasting impression on visitors. This website is a place to share what I can do with both the creative and technical aspects of web development.
